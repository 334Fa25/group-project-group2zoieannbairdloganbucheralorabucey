About Us

Company Story

Founded in 2018 by passionate horticulturist Jamie Rivers, BrightSprout Landscaping was born from a vision: to create outdoor spaces that are not only stunning but also environmentally responsible.

What started as a small design studio in Missouri has grown into a dedicated team of landscaping professionals, each bringing unique skills and creativity to every project. At BrightSprout, we believe that a landscape should work in harmony with nature supporting local ecosystems and reflecting the unique character of each property.

Whether it’s a cozy backyard garden or a vibrant commercial property, our designs enhance beauty and functionality year-round.
